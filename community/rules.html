Rules

Read all the rules that in here in currently moment.

1. Spam and flood.

1.1. Do not insert many images and stickers in a row in chats. This takes up a lot of space and hinders viewing messages.

1.2. Do not create many posts and threads in a short time, including meaningless ones. Such posts will be deleted.

1.3. Do not mention (ping) people excessively. Respect others' busyness, unavailability, or simply personal space.

2. Ads.

Advertising in this chat is generally prohibited unless you receive special permission.

Mentioning a brand or product is not advertising, however, intentional native advertising still falls under this rule.

3. Politeness etiquette.

Don't insult, call people names, or make fun of people.

3.1. If person is that your friend and he understand what is that joke, it's okay. If a person is uncomfortable with your insults, especially if thy are said in all seriousness, this is prohibited by the rules..

3.1.1. It is prohibited in any way to support/organize bullying against a person in principle.

3.2. Swears can speaks but they should be in moderation.

3.3. Do not disclose personal information unless you have a right to do so. This information may also include messages from your or others' personal correspondence, messages from private servers/chats, and so on.

3.4. Try to not a interrupt in the voice If you are asked to do so.

4. Unpleasant topics.

4.1. Don't send and no discussion unpleasant topics such as: NSFW, politics, race, sexuality of a person, etc.

4.2. Vile, unpleasant, as well as black humor and other trash are unacceptable. Publishing media with such contents is allowed only in specially designated places, specified in the community itself. Keep in mind, not every Felix_14 Community has such places.

4.3. It is prohibited to distribute and publish links to suspicious websites or materials that may contain viruses, unwanted content, as well as to fraudulent resources.

4.4. It is prohibited to distribute and publish links to any pirated content, including resources where games and other materials are published unofficially.

4.5. It is prohibited to promote illegal modification of applications, as well as their hacking and decompilation (this also applies to web resources).

4.6. Spoilers for video games, movies, and books that have appeared no earlier than two weeks ago are prohibited without hiding and without warning.

5. Contact with moderations.

5.1. If you're caught breaking the rules, be accountable for your words. Try not to avoid responsibility.

5.2. Do not provoke or encourage other people to violate the rules or commit any other bad deeds.

6. Comply with Terms of Service and rules of the social network or web resource.

7. Follow special rules, if such exists. In some places rules are wriitten differently or have extra rules. These rules overlap, but don't replace general rules of Felix_14 Community.
